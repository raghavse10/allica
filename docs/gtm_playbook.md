# GTM Playbook (Excerpt)

**North star:** Book qualified meetings and save operator hours by automating repeatable steps.  
**Tone:** pragmatic, respectful, jargon-light.  
**First touch:** 110–170 words; one clear CTA; reference a real business need inferred from enrichment.

**Guidelines**
- Never claim guaranteed approval or exact approval timelines.
- If pricing/terms are referenced, include: _"Subject to status and credit checks. Terms apply."_
- Use the company name and a plausible need based on enrichment (capacity, cash-flow, expansion, refinancing).

**Sector hints**
- Manufacturing: asset-backed lending, invoice finance options.
- Professional practices: onboarding speed, dedicated account managers.
- Food & beverage: predictable repayments and seasonal cash-flow options.
- Healthcare: flexible equipment finance, practice growth support.
- Logistics: fleet financing, working capital for expansion.
- Construction: project-based lending, equipment and property development finance.

**Email Best Practices**
- Start with a personalized greeting using the contact's name.
- Reference specific business context from enrichment (sector, size, incorporation date).
- Focus on one primary value proposition aligned with their notes/sector.
- Include exactly one clear call-to-action (book a call, reply for more info).
- Keep total length between 110-170 words for optimal engagement.
- Avoid: sales jargon, multiple CTAs, guarantees, specific timelines.

**Common Use Cases by Revenue Band**
- £500k-£2m: Equipment upgrades, seasonal working capital, single-asset finance.
- £2m-£10m: Invoice finance, refinancing, expansion capital, fleet upgrades.
- £10m+: Structured facilities, property development, acquisition finance.

**Routing Logic**
- High ICP score (>0.5): Growth-Inbound team, priority response.
- Medium ICP score (0.3-0.5): Growth-Inbound team, standard flow.
- Low ICP score (<0.3): Triage team for qualification.
- Red flags present: Manual review required before outreach.
