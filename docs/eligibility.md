# Eligibility & Compliance Notes (Excerpt)

## Basic Eligibility
- UK-based limited companies only (sole traders → manual review).
- Typical revenues: £1m–£50m; under 2 years trading → alternate flow.
- Companies under £500k revenue → usually not in target segment.
- Maximum consideration for companies up to £50m turnover.

## Red Flags & Exclusions
- Red flags: CCJs, active dissolution, mismatched directors.
- County Court Judgments (CCJs) in last 12 months require escalation.
- Companies in administration or liquidation: automatic decline.
- Significant director changes in last 6 months: additional due diligence.

## Prohibited Language
- Disallowed language: guarantees, exact approval times, teaser rates.
- Never promise "guaranteed approval" or "approval within 24/48 hours."
- Avoid specific interest rates unless formally quoted after assessment.
- Don't claim "lowest rates" or "best terms" without verification.

## Compliance Requirements
**Required disclaimer** when referencing pricing/terms:  
_Subject to status and credit checks. Terms apply._

- When uncertain about eligibility, propose a discovery call instead of offering terms.
- Always verify company status via Companies House before making offers.
- PII (personal identifiable information) must be redacted from logs and traces.
- Email addresses and phone numbers should not appear in diagnostic logs.

## Sector-Specific Guidance
- Healthcare professionals: verify professional registration and practice ownership.
- Construction: assess project pipeline and contract stability.
- Food & beverage: consider seasonality and regulatory compliance.
- Manufacturing: evaluate asset base for secured lending.
- Technology/startups: typically outside core SME lending criteria without revenue history.

## Documentation Requirements
- Minimum 2 years of accounts for standard products.
- Recent management accounts (within 6 months) for larger facilities.
- Proof of business ownership and director identification.
- Asset valuations required for asset-backed lending.
